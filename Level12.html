    Practice Plan (to be done with a partner):

    Basic Drills

    1. 2 on 1 tilt basics 10x
    2. 2 on 1 tilt from him being flat 10x
    3. 2 on 1 tilt from crab ride (adjustment drill) 3x each side, 10x
    4. 2 on 1 tilt from from rear standing 10x
    5. 2 on 1 tilt roll through version 10x
    6. Stopping the jump over 10x
    7. Escaping the jump over 10x
    8. 2 on 1 tilt to ankle lace entry jacobs and jacobs hook 10x
    9. 2 on 1 tilt to gable 10x
    10. How to flatten someone out during the gable 5x each
    11. Gable to cement mixer 10x
    12. Gable to leg bar 10x
    13. Rapid titling drill 10x

    Complex Drills

    1. Climb behind, into rear standing 2on1 tilt, into ankle lace entry, into jacob’s, into jacob’s hook 10x
    2. Duck under or shrug, into rear standing 2on1 tilt, into gable, into any gable finish.
