1. Half Nelson with sink-it-deep finish Looped
  • Did they use the mat sandwich to get their opponents head down?
  • Did they start with a half that was deep around the neck (fingers approaching the throat)?
  • Did they start with good pressure, cheek to cheek, butt high in the air (partner should not be able to lift his head off the mat)?
  • Do they sink it deep (dropping elbow to the mat)?
  • Did they crunch the spine, by squeezing their elbows together, forcing their partner’s nose towards his chest?
  • Did they move to a stack first?
  • Did they finish with their post leg up?
  • Does the final position look completely immobilizing for the bottom wrestler?

  • Did the wrestler step-up, move to sit up, then push-up with the opposite arm?
  • Did the wrestler tuck tightly in the sit-out position?

    8. How to defend a cross face Looped
  • Was the wrestler’s starting posture on bottom good?
  • Did they move their arm out of the way in time?
  • Did they shorten their neck, lifting their shoulders to their ears?
    		•
    9. Clear your wrists when in a sitout Looped
  • Did the wrestler know how to beat a cross wrist? (box out to break the grip)
  • Did the wrestler know how to beat a 1on1? (roll wrist)
  • Was the wrestler able to fully box-out his opponent?
    		•
    10. Maintaining your base while your partner tries to break you down Looped
  • Did the wrestler keep a low center of gravity?
  • Were their arms slightly bent?
    		•
    11. T-rex position Looped
  • Did the wrestler start with their elbows in? (to avoid peak-outs?)
  • Was the wrestler using his forearms to push the opponent’s arms towards one another?
  • Was the wrestler on his knees, with his toes uncurled? (to avoid them from driving into him and getting to their legs?)
  • Did the wrestler first bring his chin over one arm-pit?
  • Did the wrestler reach around the head to get to the opposite arm-pit?
  • Did the wrestler attack the hip (not reach around the waist) to finish the move?
